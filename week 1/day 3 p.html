Steps on how to make a Mug Cake

Made by Lian Canlas

Here are the following ingredients you will need to make a mug cake:

  • 4 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, combine 4 tablespoons of all-purpose flour, 4 tablespoons of sugar, 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder, 1/4 teaspoon of baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Mix well.
  2. Add 3 tablespoons of milk, 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil, and 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Stir until smooth and well combined.
  3. Optional: You can add chocolate chips, nuts, or a spoonful of peanut butter to the batter for extra flavor.
  4. Microwave the mug on high for about 1 minute and 30 seconds to 2 minutes, or until the cake has risen and is set in the middle. Cooking times may vary depending on your microwave's wattage.
  5. Carefully remove the mug from the microwave (it will be hot!) and let it cool for a minute or two before enjoying your delicious mug cake.
