iPhone 17 Review: Features, Pros & Cons

Everything you need to know before buying the iPhone 17.

Overview

The iPhone 17 is Apple’s latest flagship smartphone featuring cutting-edge technology, enhanced cameras, and an even sleeker design.

Key Features

  • 6.8-inch Super Retina XDR Display with ProMotion
  • A19 Bionic Chip for lightning-fast performance
  • Advanced Quad-Camera System with AI-enhanced photography
  • iOS 21 with new multitasking and customization options
  • Longer battery life and faster charging support

Pros

  • Outstanding camera quality with improved night mode
  • Ultra-fast performance for apps, gaming, and multitasking
  • High-resolution display with smooth scrolling and vivid colors
  • Better battery life than previous models
  • Enhanced AI features for photography and user experience

Cons

  • Premium price might not be suitable for everyone
  • Minimal external design changes from iPhone 16
  • Limited USB-C features for some users

Conclusion

The iPhone 17 is a major upgrade for users who prioritize camera performance, speed, and cutting-edge technology. It’s perfect for tech enthusiasts, though the price may not justify upgrading for casual users.
